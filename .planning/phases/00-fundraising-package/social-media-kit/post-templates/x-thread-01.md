## Tweet 2/6

The problem:

The US substance abuse treatment industry is worth $42 billion. There is no verified review system. SAMHSA lists ~17,000 facilities with no quality data. The FTC has documented predatory patient brokering where referral fees reach $10,000 per admission.

People in crisis deserve better.

## Tweet 3/6

What we're building:

- Authenticated-anonymous reviews (verified attendance, displayed anonymously)
- Transparency scores (facilities earn trust, not buy it)
- Funding finder with eligibility matching
- AI matching grounded in verified data
- Nonprofit. Zero referral fees. Zero pay-to-rank.

## Tweet 4/6

The tech:

We're building on @OriginTrail's Decentralized Knowledge Graph (DKG) so that facility data, reviews, and outcomes live on infrastructure no single party controls.

Governance through a DAO -- community-owned.
