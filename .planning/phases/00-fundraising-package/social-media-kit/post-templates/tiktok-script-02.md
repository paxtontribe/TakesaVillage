# TikTok Script: What If Facilities Couldn't Hide?

**Platform:** TikTok
**Format:** 45-second talking-head or voiceover video
**Target audience:** Families, young adults, advocates
**CTA:** Link in bio -- takesavillage.health
**Age restriction:** 18+ (substance-adjacent content)

## Script

**[HOOK -- 0-5 seconds]**
What if treatment facilities couldn't pay to hide bad reviews?

**[PROBLEM -- 5-20 seconds]**
Right now, facilities can pay to suppress negative reviews on major platforms. They can buy fake positive reviews. And some "treatment directories" charge thousands of dollars per referral -- routing people to whoever pays the most, not whoever helps the most.

When someone you love needs help, the information you find online might literally be bought and paid for.

**[SOLUTION -- 20-40 seconds]**
We're building Takes a Village -- a nonprofit treatment transparency platform where:

Every review is verified -- the reviewer actually attended.
Reviews can't be paid away or suppressed.
Facilities earn trust through transparency scores, not marketing budgets.
And all of this data lives on decentralized infrastructure that no single company controls.

**[CTA -- 40-45 seconds]**
We're pre-launch. Join the waitlist -- link in bio. Because everyone deserves trustworthy treatment information.
