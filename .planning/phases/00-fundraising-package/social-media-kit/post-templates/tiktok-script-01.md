# TikTok Script: 17,000 Facilities, No Verified Reviews

**Platform:** TikTok
**Format:** 60-second talking-head or voiceover video
**Target audience:** Families, young adults, advocates, people researching treatment
**CTA:** Link in bio -- takesavillage.health
**Age restriction:** 18+ (substance-adjacent content)

---

## Script

**[HOOK -- 0-5 seconds]**
Did you know there are 17,000 substance abuse treatment facilities in the United States -- and there's no verified review system for any of them?

**[PROBLEM -- 5-25 seconds]**
Think about that. When you're looking for a restaurant, you can read hundreds of verified reviews. When you're making one of the most important decisions of your life -- choosing treatment for yourself or someone you love -- you get... marketing claims and unverified Google reviews.

The government's treatment locator lists facilities, but it has no reviews, no cost data, no outcome tracking. And some of those "treatment directories" you find online? They route you to whichever facility pays the highest referral fee. Not the best fit. The highest fee.

**[SOLUTION TEASER -- 25-45 seconds]**
We're building something different. Takes a Village is a nonprofit platform where every review is from someone who actually attended the facility. Where facilities earn trust through transparency, not by paying us. Where treatment data lives on infrastructure that no single company can control or manipulate.

**[CTA -- 45-60 seconds]**
We're pre-launch right now and building in public. If you believe treatment information should be trustworthy, join the waitlist. Link in bio.

And if you or someone you know needs help right now -- call 1-800-662-4357. That's the SAMHSA National Helpline. It's free, confidential, and available 24/7.

## Video Notes

- Film as talking-head (more personal) or use text overlay with voiceover
- Keep energy calm but passionate -- not salesy, not clinical
- Use captions (most TikTok is watched on mute)
- Add the SAMHSA helpline number as on-screen text during the CTA

## Suggested On-Screen Text Overlays

- 0s: "17,000 treatment facilities. 0 verified review systems."
- 15s: "Some directories route you to the highest-paying facility."
- 30s: "We're building something different."
- 50s: "Join the waitlist -- link in bio"
- 55s: "Need help now? 1-800-662-4357"

## Safe Messaging Note

- No specific drugs are named in this script
- Language is hope-forward: "we're building something different," "treatment information should be trustworthy"
- No shame-based framing -- the system is broken, not the people using it
- SAMHSA helpline is included at the end
- Content is appropriate for 18+ audience per TikTok's substance-adjacent content policy
