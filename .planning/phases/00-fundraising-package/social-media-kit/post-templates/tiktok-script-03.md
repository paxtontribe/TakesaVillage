## Script

**[HOOK -- 0-5 seconds]**
The FTC documented predatory patient brokering in the treatment industry. Here's what that means for you.

**[EXPLANATION -- 5-20 seconds]**
Patient brokering is when someone gets paid to send a person to a specific treatment facility. Not because it's the best fit -- because there's a referral fee. These fees can reach $10,000 per admission.

Some of those "helpful" treatment hotlines and directories you find online are part of this system. They look like they're helping, but they're routing you based on who pays them.

**[SOLUTION -- 20-28 seconds]**
We're building Takes a Village -- a nonprofit with zero referral fees, zero pay-to-rank, and verified reviews. Because everyone in crisis deserves honest information.

**[CTA -- 28-30 seconds]**
Link in bio to join the waitlist.
