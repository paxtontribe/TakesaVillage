## Posting Guidelines

### Timing (US Eastern Time)

| Platform | Best Times | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Instagram | 11am or 7pm | Engagement peaks at lunch and evening |
| TikTok | 10am or 7pm | Morning and evening scroll sessions |
| X | 9am or 12pm | Morning news cycle and lunch break |
| LinkedIn | 8am or 12pm | Professional morning check-in and lunch |

### Safe Messaging Guidelines (All Platforms)

Every post must follow these rules:

- Use "person with substance use disorder" -- never "addict," "junkie," "abuser," or "user"
- Frame recovery as achievable -- hope-forward messaging always
- Do not describe how people obtain drugs or substances
- Do not use imagery that could glorify substance use
- Include SAMHSA helpline (1-800-662-4357) or 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline in at least one pinned/highlighted post per platform
- Avoid shame-based language -- "You deserve better information" not "Don't let this happen to you"
- When discussing statistics, contextualize with hope -- "This is why we're building something better"
- Use content warnings where appropriate (especially TikTok and Instagram)
